Our verification process uses 5 main categories

With these 5 categories we can with a high degree of accuracy create a rental score that can predict whether or not a tenant can default on payments

Payment history

We track your tenant’s payments across multiple rental units. This is the most important metric

Defaulted bills

We track if your tenant has any defaulted payments in any rental unit they are paying for.

Employment

We track if your tenant is employed and if they can afford to make rental payments

Rental history

We collect the tenant’s previous rental history and view how they were rated by their previous property managers

Rental length

We view how long the tenant has been renting and how viable they are as a tenant

Our rental scores are divided into 4 main sections

After verification we provide your tenant with a rental score that determines where they fall. The rental score is from 0 - 1000. We update your tenant’s score monthly automatically.
